# Sikh Empire: Ranjit Singh

This mod is tuned as a high-power military/faith/food civilization for Civilization VI with Rise & Fall and Gathering Storm.

## Core Design

- Civilization: Sikh Empire
- Leader: Maharaja Ranjit Singh
- Civilization ability: Chardi Kala
- Leader ability: Miri-Piri
- Unique unit: Akali Nihang, replacing Man-at-Arms
- Unique unit: Misldar Cavalry, replacing Cuirassier
- Unique building: Gurdwara Sahib, replacing Temple

## Balance Intent

The civ is intentionally top-tier. It has a strong river start, early farm faith scaling, a strong Holy Site economy, military production, faith-on-kills from Miri-Piri, and two heavily tuned unique units. Nihang and Misldar Cavalry both ignore combat penalties from being wounded.

## Religion Combat Note

Civilization VI combat units expose Spain's native "opponent is other religion" combat hook, but not a stable database requirement for "opponent follows a religion with more total followers than mine." The mod therefore keeps the functional Firaxis-supported rule: +5 Combat Strength against units of a civilization following a rival majority religion. This appears in the combat preview and avoids unsupported requirement rows.

## Flood Note

Gathering Storm exposes full river flood avoidance through Egypt's native modifier but does not expose a safe fractional "50% damage reduction" modifier for districts and improvements. The full immunity modifier has been removed so Chardi Kala no longer exceeds the original balance target.
